My name is Roydon Jeffrey and I am an inspiring IOS and Web Developer who recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course. My journey as a developer started in December, 2014 when I first found out about computer programming. I had never saw a line of code nor did I know that it existed. My emotions was too powerful for me to even control, so I dove right into it and never looked back since. The fact that I can make a computer do almost anything I want it to do with just a few words and symbols is the most blind blowing thing to me. Sometimes I feel guilty and hold myself responsible for not learning about this amazing skill much earlier in life. But since I can't do anything about it now, I work with a chip on my shoulders to try and catch up for all the lost years I could have been programming. Even though that's not possible, I like to believe that it is. All I ask is that you give me a chance and I'll show you what I can do with a little training and guidance.
